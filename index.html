Welcome to Slammers


Slammers Beach Volleyball is a locally owned company aimed at providing the community with a friendly and enjoyable experience of beach Volleyball. It's not a heavy impact sport but can still provide good fitness and social interaction, making it an exceptional choice for people of many different ages and suiting those with busy personal agendas.
Previous month's scores and points allocated for each existing team can be found on the Announcements page.
